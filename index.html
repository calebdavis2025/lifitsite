This Privacy Policy explains how LiFit (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile applications, website, and related services (the “Services”). By using the Services, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following categories:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and profile details you provide when creating an account.
  • Workout & App Activity: Exercises, workout logs, plan usage, stats, and interactions within the app.
  • User Content: Photos, videos, and posts you upload for social or profile features.
  • Messages: Direct messages, comments, and social interactions (if enabled).
  • Location Data: Approximate location if you enable features like friend discovery or gym discovery.
  • Device & Technical Data: Device identifiers, IP address, crash logs, diagnostics, and performance data.
  • Analytics & Cookies: In-app analytics, usage metrics, and web cookies used for improving functionality.
  • Advertising Data: If you use the free tier, limited data may be collected for delivering in-app ads.

2. Use of Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide core workout tracking and social features
  • Sync your data across devices through cloud storage
  • Show friend, gym, and community content
  • Provide recommendations and personalized features
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Communicate announcements or support messages
  • Enforce safety, prevent misuse, and maintain account security

3. Tracking Disclosure (Apple Requirement)

LiFit does not track you across third-party apps or websites.

We do not sell personal data, and we do not share data with advertisers for cross-app tracking purposes.

If you upload photos or videos, LiFit stores them for social posting, profile features, and app functionality. Your media may be visible to other users depending on your in-app visibility settings. You may delete your content at any time.

5. Location Information

We only collect location data if you enable optional features such as gym discovery or nearby user features. You may disable location permissions at any time in your device settings, but certain features may not work.

6. Third-Party Services We Use

We use third-party service providers to operate the Services. These services may process or store data on our behalf:

  • Firebase Authentication – account login and identity
  • Firestore Database – storing workouts, posts, and app data
  • Firebase Storage – storing photos and media
  • Firebase Analytics – app analytics and performance metrics

These providers are contractually required to protect your information and comply with Apple’s App Store requirements.

7. Data Sharing

We only share your information with:

  • Vendors and service providers that support our app infrastructure
  • Legal authorities if required to comply with the law
  • Other users when you choose to post publicly or interact socially
  • Parties you authorize or direct us to share information with

8. Data Linked to You

The following data may be linked to your identity within the app:

  • Email address
  • Profile information
  • Workout data and logs
  • Social posts and photos
  • App activity
  • Device identifiers

9. Data Not Linked to You

The following may be collected but are not linked to your account:

  • Crash logs
  • Aggregate analytics
  • Performance diagnostics

10. Data Retention & Security

We retain information only as long as necessary to operate the Services or meet legal obligations. We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data, but no method of transmission is completely secure.

11. Your Rights & Choices

You may:

LiFit does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or the minimum age in your region). If we learn a child has used the Services, we will delete the account and associated data.
